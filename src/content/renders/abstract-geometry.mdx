## Process

This piece was created using Blender's geometry nodes to generate parametric surfaces. The surface is defined by the equation:

$$
\mathbf{r}(u, v) = \begin{pmatrix} (2 + \cos v) \cos u \\ (2 + \cos v) \sin u \\ \sin v \end{pmatrix}
$$

where $u, v \in [0, 2\pi)$.

## Technical Details

- Geometry nodes for surface generation
- Principled BSDF with metallic finish
- HDRI environment lighting
- Exported as GLB for web viewing
